3 Testing Documents

The google form: In our questionnaire we started of by asking our respondent some questions in an effort to screen our participants, chiefly question 1-4. In our screening we wanted to clarify certain aspects of the respondent mainly in regard to his/her experience browsing the web. The following questions are more important as they give actual feedback as to how the respondent experienced the website. We provide four questions where to respondent chooses one of five numbers expressing their experience. Finally, we gave the respondent the oppertunity to tell us what kind of device they used when browsing the website and what browser, followed by a text-field where the respondent can provide a more detailed feedback if so desired. The two latter questions were not required, as we did not want to force our respondents to provide written feedback.

4 Tester Audience

In in our quest for respondents within our clients desired customer group, we opted for an approach in which we shared the form on various social platforms like Facebook, with people we know ourselves to be qualify as probable users in the future. This approach yielded decent results as we were able to get 22 responders. As far as responses are concerned, the result are as follows:

  1. How old are you?

  2. How confident are you with browsing, shopping, or other online-related tasks?

  3. Which device(s) do you usually use for browsing?

  4. Have you used similar sites before?

  5. Did you find the website easy to navigate?

  6. Was the booking feature intuitive?

  7. Were you able to understand the menu?

  8. How was your overall experience with the site?

5 Testing Platforms

Our website was exposed to several devices and browsers, the fact that our website seemingly worked quite well as can be seen from the results of the questionnaire. We were exposed to devices like iphone, Samsung Galaxy, Huawei p30, Mac, PC, s and ipad's, and browsers such as chrome, safari, edge and explorer. All in all, our site was tested on many of the most popular platforms and fared quite well, hence we can with resonable confidence conclude that our site performs well on the most important platforms.
